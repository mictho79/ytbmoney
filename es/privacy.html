Privacy Policy

How we handle your data at YouTube Money Calculator.

Last updated: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how YouTube Money Calculator ("we", "our", or "the site"), accessible at https://ytbearnings.com, collects, uses, and protects any information when you use our website.

1. Overview

YouTube Money Calculator is a static website. The calculator itself runs entirely in your browser — no calculation data, views, or revenue estimates are sent to or stored on any server. We do not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information to use the calculator.

2. Information We May Collect

As a static website, we collect minimal data. The following may be collected automatically:

2.1 Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics tools (such as Cloudflare Web Analytics or similar) to understand general traffic patterns. This may include:

  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • General geographic region (country level, not city)
  • Browser type and device type
  • Referral source (how you found the site)

Analytics data is aggregated and anonymized. We do not collect personally identifiable information through analytics.

2.2 Hosting & Infrastructure

This site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may process standard server log data (including IP addresses) as part of their infrastructure services. Please refer to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle this data.

2.3 Cookies

YouTube Money Calculator does not set any first-party tracking cookies. The site may use browser local storage to remember calculator settings within a session. No persistent cookies are used to track you across visits or third-party sites.

If third-party analytics tools are added in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly, and any cookie consent mechanism required by law will be implemented.

3. How We Use Information

Any data collected is used solely to:

  • Understand which pages and features are most useful to visitors
  • Identify and fix technical errors
  • Improve the calculator's functionality and content

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites (such as YouTube Studio, Google Analytics documentation, or other resources). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

5. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

6. Data Security

Since this is a static website that does not collect personal data or operate user accounts, the risk of a data breach is minimal. The site is served over HTTPS to protect data in transit.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding personal data including:

  • The right to access data we hold about you
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of analytics tracking

Since we collect minimal data and none of it is personally identifiable in isolation, most of these rights are satisfied by default. For any data-related requests, please contact us at the address below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
