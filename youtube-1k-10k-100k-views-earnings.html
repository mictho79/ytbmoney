Why Your Earnings Differ From Estimates

These tables provide useful benchmarks, but your actual earnings will vary. Common reasons for divergence:

  • International traffic: If 60% of your views are from India at $0.80 RPM, your effective RPM is much lower than the US benchmark in these tables
  • Ad blocker usage: Typically 15–30% of desktop users block ads; mobile has lower ad block rates but also lower CPMs
  • Monetization not enabled on older videos: If some videos aren't monetized, they don't contribute to earnings
  • Season: January RPM is 20–40% below December RPM for the same content
  • Channel age: Newer channels often see lower RPMs as AdSense lacks sufficient audience targeting data

How to Increase Earnings Per Video

The most effective ways to increase earnings from the same number of views:

  • Enable all ad formats — Non-skippable and mid-roll ads significantly increase revenue per view on 8+ minute videos
  • Improve audience geography — Create content that appeals to US/UK/CA audiences to attract higher CPM advertisers
  • Optimize for content-advertiser match — Include relevant keywords in titles and descriptions to attract premium advertisers in your niche
  • Make longer videos — 8–15 minute videos earn 2–3× more per view than 3–5 minute videos due to mid-roll ad slots
  • Post in Q4 — Same video, same views, 30–60% more earnings in Q4 vs Q1
