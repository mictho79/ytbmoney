Basketball YouTube RPM & Earnings 2026

Typical Basketball channel RPM runs $1.50 – $5.50 in 2026 mid RPM. A channel averaging 1M monthly views earns around $3,000.00 per month from AdSense alone.

Basketball sits in the sports RPM sweet spot: the NBA drives a US-heavy audience (38% of global viewership) while maintaining massive international reach through stars like Luka, Giannis, and Wembanyama. The result is a typical channel RPM of $3 — roughly 2× what soccer channels earn, though still well below NFL or MMA rates.

Why Basketball Channels Earn Mid RPM

US-heavy audience boosts CPM

With 38% of views coming from the US and another 6% from Canada, basketball channels capture premium advertising inventory. Auto, telecom, and sports-betting advertisers compete for North American basketball audiences, pushing CPMs to $7–$15 for US-viewed inventory.

Philippines is the RPM drag

The Philippines is the #2 basketball-watching country globally per NBA international data, but Philippine CPMs average $0.80–$2.00. A channel heavily dependent on PH viewership will see blended RPMs closer to the $1.50–$2 range even with strong US traffic.

Sneaker economy pays premium

Sneaker review and basketball culture channels benefit from Nike, Adidas, Puma, and Anta advertising — often running branded segments at $0.03–$0.08 per view (on top of AdSense). This effectively doubles or triples total revenue for well-positioned creators.

FAQ — Basketball YouTube Earnings

What is a realistic basketball channel RPM?

Most basketball channels earn $2.50–$4 RPM with a mixed audience. Pure NBA highlights channels trend lower ($2–$3) due to copyright claims. Analysis/film channels with US-skewed audiences can reach $4–$6.

Do highlights channels get demonetized?

NBA match footage is protected by Turner and the NBA itself. Most highlight channels operate under Content ID claims that share or divert revenue. Fair-use analysis channels (with commentary and selective clips) typically retain more earnings.

Are basketball Shorts profitable?

Basketball Shorts see Shorts RPMs of $0.06–$0.15 — higher than the global Shorts average because of the US-heavy audience. Still, long-form earns 20–40× more per view than Shorts.

How do basketball creators scale income?

Sneaker brand deals, NBA 2K partnerships, fantasy-sports app sponsorships, and Patreon film-breakdown tiers are the primary growth levers beyond AdSense.
