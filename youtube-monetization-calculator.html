YouTube Partner Program Requirements 2026

To join the YouTube Partner Program and start earning ad revenue, your channel must meet all of the following requirements simultaneously:

  • 1,000 subscribers — Your channel must have at least 1,000 subscribers at the time of application.
  • 4,000 public watch hours — Accumulated in the past rolling 12 months. Private and unlisted videos don't count. Shorts views don't count.
  • No active Community Guidelines strikes — Your channel must be in good standing with no active strikes.
  • 2-step verification — Your associated Google Account must have 2-step verification enabled.
  • AdSense account — You need a valid AdSense account linked to your YouTube channel.

Alternative path for Shorts creators: 10 million Shorts views in 90 days (plus 500 subscribers) can qualify you for the YouTube Partner Program Lite tier.

How to Reach 4,000 Watch Hours Faster

Watch hours accumulate from every minute viewers spend watching your videos. Here's how to accelerate your progress:

  • Create longer content: A 15-minute video with 300 views generates 75 hours of watch time. A 3-minute video with the same views generates only 15 hours.
  • Improve audience retention: The longer viewers watch, the more watch hours you accumulate. Strong hooks and engaging pacing are critical.
  • Create playlist series: Playlists increase session watch time as viewers auto-play multiple videos back to back.
  • Optimize for search: Videos discovered via YouTube search tend to have higher retention rates than those found via recommendations.
  • Upload consistently: Regular uploads give you more opportunities to accumulate watch hours each week.

What Happens After Joining YPP?

Once approved for YPP, you gain access to multiple monetization features:

  • Ad Revenue: Earn from skippable ads, non-skippable ads, bumper ads, and overlay ads shown on your videos.
  • YouTube Premium Revenue: Earn a share of YouTube Premium subscription fees based on watch time from Premium members.
  • Channel Memberships: Offer monthly memberships to your subscribers for exclusive perks (requires 500+ subscribers).
  • Super Thanks & Super Chat: Receive tips from viewers during livestreams and on regular videos.
  • Shopping: Connect your store and tag products directly in your videos.

Most creators see their first AdSense payment within 2–3 months of joining YPP, once their balance reaches the $100 payment threshold.
